# **App Name**: Apex Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Project Gallery: Showcase your projects with images, descriptions, and links.
- Detailed Project View: Provide in-depth information for each project, including technologies used, challenges, and outcomes.
- About Me Section: Introduce yourself with a professional bio, headshot, and personal statement.
- Skills Showcase: Visually represent your skills with progress bars and descriptions of your expertise.
- Contact Form: Enable visitors to easily contact you through a simple form that sends messages directly to your email.
- Dark Mode Toggle: Switch between light and dark themes for user preference and accessibility.
- AI Content Generation: Use an AI tool to generate personalized introductions or project descriptions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) for highlights and interactive elements.
- Background color: Dark charcoal grey (#1A1A1A) to provide a modern, sophisticated feel.
- Accent color: Deep purple (#6A0DAD) for subtle contrast and visual interest.
- Body text and headlines: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif with a modern, machined look, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use a set of simple line icons in electric blue to represent different skills and project categories.
- Employ a grid-based layout to ensure a clean and organized presentation of projects and information.
- Incorporate subtle animations on hover and page transitions to enhance user engagement.
